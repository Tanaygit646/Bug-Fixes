Magudi

Origins and History:

The Magudi is a traditional wind instrument associated with snake charming in India.

Instrument Features:

It is a simple reed instrument that produces a distinctively eerie sound.

Musical Role:

The Magudi is often used in folk performances and rituals.

Conclusion

The Magudi remains an iconic part of Indian musical heritage.
