Surbahar

Origins and History:

The Subahar is a traditional Indian string instrument, similar to the sitar but with deeper tones.

Instrument Features:

It is designed for playing classical Indian music, particularly in meditative and slow-tempo compositions.

Musical Role:

The Subahar is known for its resonant and rich sound, often used in Hindustani classical music.

Conclusion

The Subahar remains a significant instrument in Indian classical traditions, valued for its expressive sound.
