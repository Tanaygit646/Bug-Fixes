Saragani

Origins and History:

The Saragani is a traditional string instrument used in folk and classical music.

Instrument Features:

It consists of a wooden resonator with gut or metal strings, played with a bow.

Musical Role:

Used in various regional music traditions, producing a rich and expressive tone.

Conclusion

The Saragani remains an important instrument in traditional Indian and folk music.
